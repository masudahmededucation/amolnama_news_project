Shared / Modular Components — Newshub Forms
=============================================
Last updated: 2026-03-22

RULE: Never duplicate these. Always reuse via include or factory call.
RULE: Case-insensitive comparison — always use .toUpperCase() or .toLowerCase() when comparing status_code or dataset.code.

1. GD/FIR Toggle (General Diary / First Information Report)
   Template: law-gd-fir-section.html (include with fir_prefix="legal" or "wcv")
   JS:       news-law-gd-fir.js (factory: window.newshubLawGdFir.initLawGdFirSection({ prefix, onChange }))
   Used by:  crime-legal-form, extortion-legal-form, land-grab-legal-form, wcv-legal-form
   Data:     Each form provides <script id="{prefix}-fir-status-data"> with fir_statuses_json
   IDs:      {prefix}-fir-status-radios, {prefix}-fir-details-row, {prefix}-case-number,
             {prefix}-police-station, {prefix}-police-refused-row, {prefix}-no-fir-row
