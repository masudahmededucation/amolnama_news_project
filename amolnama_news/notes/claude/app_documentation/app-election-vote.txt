## Introduction
Election voting system with digital ballot management, candidate nomination,
voter eligibility checking, and multi-level results drillthrough
(national → division → district → constituency).

## Features

### Voting
- Check voter eligibility before casting
- Digital ballot with candidate list
- One vote per eligible voter

### Results Drillthrough
- National → Division → District → Constituency hierarchy
- Party-wise vote counts with percentages
- Past election results browsing

## Key Functions

### Views (views.py)
- home() — landing with current election info
- check_eligibility() — voter eligibility verification
- cast_vote() — ballot submission
- past_results_drillthrough() — multi-level results navigation
- _build_national_results() — aggregate national vote counts
- _build_division_results() — division-level breakdown
- _build_district_results() — district-level breakdown
- _build_constituency_results() — constituency-level breakdown
- _calculate_percentages() — vote percentage calculation
- _group_by_location() — group results by location hierarchy
