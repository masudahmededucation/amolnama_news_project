Core features (Phase 1):
- Rich text biography (Quill editor — already available)
- Cover image + photo gallery (existing blog pattern)
- YouTube video links (existing pattern)
- Category filter pills (DB-driven)
- Actions bar (like/bookmark/share — shared component)
- Subject info: name, birth/death dates, birthplace, occupation
- Bengali slug for SEO

== Phase 2 (future) ==

1. The Storyteller's Workshop (Creation & Editing Suite)
   A. Rich Text Editor — block-based (Gutenberg/Notion style)
      - Formatting: Bold, Italic, Underline, Strikethrough, Hyperlinks
      - Headings: H1, H2, H3 for sectioning (Early Life, Career, Family)
      - Block Types: Lists, blockquotes, separators
      - Drafting & Revisions: autosave + revision history

   B. Comprehensive Audio Integration
      - Direct audio upload (MP3, WAV, AAC)
      - In-app recording via microphone
      - Custom audio player (play/pause, waveform, seek bar)
      - Transcription support (optional AI transcription)

   C. Video Integration & Display
      - Direct video upload (MP4/MOV)
      - Embed support (YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion)
      - Custom video player with captions/subtitles (VTT/SRT)

   D. Supporting Media & Documents
      - Inline images within text flow
      - Photo galleries grouped by life period
      - Document uploads (certificates, letters, news clippings as PDF/image)

2. Structuring and Navigating
   A. Dynamic Timeline View
      - Interactive vertical/horizontal timeline
      - Auto-generated from key dates
      - Click milestone → jump to chapter

   B. Posthumous Tributes / In Memoriam
      - Dedicated memoriam biography type
      - Collaborative creation (multiple authors)
      - Tributes guestbook (moderated, separate from comments)

   C. Content Categorization
      - Pre-defined categories + custom tags
      - Era/decade filtering
      - Origin location filtering

3. Search, Discovery, and Engagement
   A. Advanced Search — multivariable filtering
   B. Moderated comments
   C. Bookmark/reading list
   D. Professional sharing (email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn)

== Inspiration ==
- StoryCorps — oral histories (text + audio)
- Medium — clean block-based editor
- Ancestry.com / MyHeritage — timelines, documents, family collaboration
- Online memoriam sites — tribute guestbooks, co-authoring
